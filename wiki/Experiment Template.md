# Experiment Template

Use this template for each prototype page.

## Thesis

One sentence describing the claim this prototype tests.

## Context

What idea, failure, or previous prototype led to this experiment?

Related concepts:

## Design Sketch

Describe:

- the source-level description
- the semantic core or intermediate representation
- the interpreters or handlers
- the generated artefact, if any
- what should erase before runtime

## Prototype Boundary

In scope:

- the minimum implementation needed to test the claim

Out of scope:

- product polish
- broad language completeness
- abstractions not under evaluation

- what would count as failure

Record what happened, including awkward parts.

## Theory Revision

What should change in the project theory?

Options:

- keep the claim
- narrow the claim
- reject the claim
